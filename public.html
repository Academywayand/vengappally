Shamsul Ulama Public School (SUPS)

Shamsul Ulama Public School (SUPS) established in 2008, situated in landscape of Vengappally, Kalpetta, provides synthesis of material education and islamic education with well disciplined worship practices. The school adhere to CBSE in material education, whereas Samastha Kerala Vidhyabyasa Board in islamic studies. The school assures a stimulating atmosphere for curriculum and extra - curriculum activities with its proficient teachers and infrastructures.
