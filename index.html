SHAMSUL ULAMA ISLAMIC ACADEMY VENGAPPALLY KALPETTA

ACADEMY VENGAPPALLY

Shamsul Ulama Islamic Academy (SUIA) is a group of institutions established by SKSSF Wayanad District Committee on August 26 in 2002 with the mission to mould the talented future generations and impart the Islamic and Arts education in the district.

About us

Educational institutions run by skssf wayanad district committee

Institutions of wafy, hifzul Quran, Jamia Arabia junior College, public school and two mahadhiya colleges.

Most of the institutions are affiliated with CIC, Jamia nooriya Arabic college and Darul Huda Islamic University

Safe secure and women-friendly campus for women's education.

Recognised by samastha Kerala jammiyathul ulama.

Primary education facilities for KG level students with Islamic disciplinary environment.

All Institution Details

Shamsul Ulama Islamic Academy

In 2002, Shamsul ulama Islamic Academy has established by reckon contribution of several scholars as an honorary service to the society under skssc district committee in vengapally Wayanad.

SAADA Islamic & Arts Collage Varambatta

Founded in 2008 to empower students from their young age itself with Islamic discipline. the institution is affiliated with Jamia nooriya Arabic college.

Shamsul Ulama Public School Vengappally

Shamsul Ulama Public School (SUPS) established in 2008, situated in landscape of Vengappally, Kalpetta

Umer Ali Shihab Thangal Memmorial Hifzul Quran Collage Vengappally

Umarali Shihab Thangal Hifz Quran College, founded in 2007, beslowes great opportunity for memorizing the holy Quran

Shamsul Ulama Islamic Arts & Mahdiyya Womens Collage

Women's College In 2016 shamsul ulama women's college was established in kalpetta and provides a 'Mahdiyya' course affiliated with Darul Huda Islamic University.

Shihab Thangal Memmorial Mahdiyya Womens Collage

Women's College In 2010 Shihab Thangal Memmorial Mahdiyya Womens Collage was established in bathery and provides a 'Mahdiyya' course affiliated with Darul Huda Islamic University.

Vision

Enriches the society with proficient scholars who possess capabilities of tackling Islamic knowledge along with contemporary studies and guides the Muslim community. Upholds the glory of Islam and the heritage of Muslims. educates and empowers Muslim women socially and intellectually within the religious circle. Erects the new generation in an Islamic environment. Strengthens social cohesion in a pluralistic society.

Misson

Provides integrated education Encourages cognitive transfers based on traditional values. Promotes Qur'anic related education. Creates a learning environment based on religious disciplinary values for both boys and girls separately Establishes public education based on moral values Captivate premise of muslims by implementing social and cultural projects among them.
