SHAMSUL ULAMA ISLAMIC ACADEMY

In 2002, Shamsul ulama Islamic Academy has established by reckon contribution of several scholars as an honorary service to the society under skssc district committee in vengapally Wayanad. Academy provides integrated education of material studies and Islamic studies. the Academy is a most eminent and popular institution in the landscape of Kerala with its quality education and proficient scholars in various fields, affiliated to the coordination of Islam colleges (cic) that provides wafy course. The institution bestows particular coaching in extra curriculum activities besides curriculum activities that arise the popularity, and eminence of the institutions of every student also. more than hundreds of wafies have graduated from the institution and serving for society in various fields within the country and abroad. the Academy takes motherhood for its affiliated institution like saada, women's hifz that plays a vital role in the propagation of Islam in the society
